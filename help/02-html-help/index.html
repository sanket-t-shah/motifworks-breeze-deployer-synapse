Obtaining your User Object ID

Step 1:

Logon to your Azure Account.

 

Step 2:

Click on All services and use search text as aad. Click on Azure Active Directory from the shown list.

 

Step 3:

Click on Users in your left blade.

 

Step 4:

Click on All users in your left blade. Search for your user name and click on the same.

 

Step 5:

Copy Object ID. You'll need this in your next steps.
